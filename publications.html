  • ACM/IEEE Joint Conference on Digital Libraries(JCDL 2020)
    Analysis of the Academic Genealogy of Education,JCDL '20: Proceedings of the ACM/IEEE Joint Conference on Digital Libraries in 2020
    Kanishka Halder , Aniruddha Chattopadhyay,Debarshi Kumar Sanyal,Plaban Kumar Bhowmick,Plaban Kumar Bhowmick,Partha Pratim Das
    Academic genealogy graphs (AGGs) identify the lineage of researchers and highlight the roles of mentors and academic institutions in the evolution of a discipline. In this poster, we study EduTree, the AGG of the discipline of education. We identify the major characteristics of EduTree including the main areas of research, the pioneering institutions, and the researchers with a high mentorship index
